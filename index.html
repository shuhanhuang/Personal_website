Research Program

Tencent - Hunyuan Foundation Model Team
  • Knowledge QA Post-training with Hallucination Reduction: Leveraged SFT + RLVR and content based detection reward to optimize QA performance; improved accuracy on internal and open benchmarks about ~5-10%.
  • Knowledge-Aware Reward Modeling via Rubrics: Built a knowledge-centric reward framework using knowledge extraction and rubric-based evaluation; reducing atomic-level hallucinations significantly.
  • Adaptive Reasoning via Fast-Slow Thinking Fusion: Combined SFT and RL to train fast/slow reasoning modes and introduced difficulty-aware rewards for adaptive switching.
Bytedance - Flow Group
  • Agentic RAG via SearchRL: Trained a RAG Agent using SearchRL with LLM-as-a-Judge, format rewards, and search-based rewards; improved online accuracy by ~10%.
  • Automated Embedding Training Pipeline: Developed a full-stack pipeline (from data processing to evaluation) for BGE models; improved FAQ retrieval recall by ~8% and enabled scalable reuse across teams.
  • High-Accuracy Intent Classification with Data Feedback Loop: Trained an intent classification model via SFT and iterative bad-case mining + data synthesis; achieved 96% (HR) and 94.5% (CS) accuracy in production.
  • Topic Tree-Based Query Rewriting for RAG: Constructed hierarchical topic trees via unsupervised clustering and used them for query rewriting and dual filtering; increased interception rate by ~5% and reached 75% overall.
Baidu - ACG Group
  • Hierarchical Summarization for Medical RAG: Designed a summary-tree framework with hierarchical aggregation over 3k+ medical documents; improved RAG QA accuracy by ~20% and enhanced knowledge structuring.
  • Speech Model Fine-tuning for Medical Dialogue Systems: Fine-tuned ASR models with LoRA and deployed end-to-end systems; improved recognition accuracy from 85.1% to 98.7% and dialect recognition from 61.8% to 87.3%.

Summary

My research focuses on LLM post-training, especially reinforcement learning (RL) and supervised fine-tuning (SFT). I have extensive experience training large-scale models (200B+ parameters) and scaling training across GPU clusters with hundreds of GPUs. Beyond foundational model research, I have also led LLM fine-tuning and deployment in real-world production scenarios.
